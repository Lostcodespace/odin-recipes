Gysahl Greens Smoothie

A delectable meal best shared with a bird

Have you ever wanted to make a meal that you and your chocobo may enjoy at the same time? Well look no further than the greenest milkshake since the shamrock shake. This isnt just a drink that Eorzean's can enjoy, this is your grandmas new meal for breakfast. This drink will keep your stomach from growling and your pallet quinched while fighting even the nastiest of baddies. (That means you Ascians!)

  • One Banana
  • One piece of romaine lettuce
  • Three scoops of frozen vanilla yogurt
  • one-fourth cup of coconut milk
  • two teaspoons of matcha
  1. Put all ingredients into blender
  2. Blend previously discussed ingredients until all ingrediants are completely blended
  3. (Optional) Top with whipped cream and sprinkle on some red beans
